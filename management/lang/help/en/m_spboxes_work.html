Keyword This is the keyword with which your site will bee associate. It means that when people entered this keyword in the search box. Your link to the site will be appear in search results.
Bid This is the price which you give to the keyword. After people clicks into your url this price will be substract from your balance. Remember than higher bid than closer your link to the top of page. Use Search bids tool to check keyword popularity and see which bids are for this keyword.
URL Link to your site.
Title Title under which your link to the site will be appear.
Description Description of your site. Type here all what you want to describe your site. This description will be appear under your site title when people see results of search the keyword.
Image URL The field "Image URL" is intended for entering the image url Sponsor Box - the given image will be displayed in Search results. For the Image to be displayed correctly it must have the following dimensions 80 by 40 pixels (width by height).
Time The field "Time" is intended for choosing the interval of time (in days), during which you want the Sponsor Box displayed.
Note: This field available only when you add new Sponsor Box.
Registered The date of keyword registration.
Note: This field available for readonly when you edit Sponsor Box.
Finishing The date of Sponsor Box expire.
Note: This field available for readonly when you edit Sponsor Box.
