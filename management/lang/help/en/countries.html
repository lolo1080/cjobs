Countries Use this feature you can allow/deny countrys from which your visitors come. Set "Security Level" for locked countries to protect your site. Click "Security Level" at the top of page and choose a prefer level.
REMEMBER This Settings are global for all of users but you can personalize country protection for any user. Click "Users" then choose user. Click "Edit countries Configuration" than check "Use this countries configuration by default for this user" and lock/unlock countries for user personally.
