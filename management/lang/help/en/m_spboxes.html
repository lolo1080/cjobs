Sponsor Box is a box which you can associate with your keyword and which will be displayed on the search result page. You can use images in your boxes for a more attractive advertisment. Use your logo or product photos as images.

When this section is entered you are displayed the following information:
Keyword This is the keyword with which your site will bee associate. Keyword can contain only Latin letters, number and blank, the remaining symbols are not allowed.
Title Title under which your link to the site will be appear. Title will be displayed in Search results page.
Registered The date of keyword registration.
Finishing The date of Sponsor Box expire.
Status If listing status is "Approve"' the description will be displayed in Search results. If listing status is "Pending"' the description will be displayed in Search results after Administrator approve.
