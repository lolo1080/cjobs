and consists from 4 parts :
  1. Button "Filter" - filter using. When you click on "Filter" the selection will be done;
  2. Checkbox- filter activation on given selection field. When the button "Filter" clicked the selection will be done only for the fields where Checkbox is switched on;
  3. Text Field - for the text. The text limits the volume of showing dates;
  4. Condition - for the dates interdependences, inputted in the field in the "Text field" and showed for the user. "Conditions" can be :
